Privacy Policy

Your privacy, plainly.

iKITES Technologies Inc. ("iKITES", "we", "us") keeps a deliberately small footprint. This page explains what limited information we collect when you reach out or browse this site, how we use it, and the choices you have. Last updated 24 June 2026.

What we collect

This is a marketing site, not an application. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not run third-party advertising or behavioural-tracking scripts. The only information we receive is:

  • What you send us. When you email info@ikites.ai, we receive your email address, your message, and anything you choose to include in it.
  • Basic technical data. Like most websites, our hosting provider records standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) to keep the site secure and available.

How we use it

We use the information solely to respond to your message, to hold the conversation you started, and to operate and protect this website. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it except where strictly necessary to run the site (for example, our email and hosting providers) or where required by law.

Data retention

We keep correspondence only for as long as it is useful to the relationship you started with us, and server logs for a limited period in line with our hosting provider's defaults.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. To make any of these requests, email info@ikites.ai and we will respond personally.

Changes to this policy

As iKITES grows, this policy may change. When it does, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected here rather than communicated individually.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to info@ikites.ai.
